About BizConnect

Our mission is to connect businesses, organizations, and individuals to a wide range of professional events, conferences, workshops, and networking opportunities across the country. Whether you're an attendee looking for an engaging event or an organizer seeking the perfect venue, BizConnect offers a seamless experience to enhance your business tourism journey.

We aim to position Bosnia and Herzegovina as a top destination for business tourism by promoting the country’s rich cultural heritage, scenic landscapes, and modern infrastructure. With cities like Sarajevo, Banja Luka, Zenica, Tuzla, and Mostar leading the way, there is no shortage of amazing venues that are perfect for hosting large-scale conferences, intimate business meetings, and everything in between.
